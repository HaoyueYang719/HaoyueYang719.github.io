Personal Information

Haoyue Yang received the B.Sc degree in electrical engineering and automation from Zhejiang University of Science and Technology, Hangzhou, China, in 2016, the M.Sc degree in control science and engineering from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China, in 2020, and the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from Tongji University, Shanghai, China, in 2025. He is currently a Postdoctoral Fellow with the School of Information and Communication Engineering, Hainan University, Hainan, China. He has published more than 20 papers in international journals and conferences in the field of control, including 4 papers in IEEE Transactions (IEEE TSMC, TAES, TNSE, TAI), 5 authorized or published invention patents, and presided over a postdoctoral research project in Hainan Province.
